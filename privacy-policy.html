**1. Intro**

We're serious about keeping your data safe and private. By using our stuff, you're saying you've read and get this Privacy Policy.

**2. Info We Grab**

We grab info to make our stuff work, make it better, and keep it secure. Here's the lowdown:

**a. Info You Give Us**

You give us info when you:

*   Make an account
*   Buy something or sign up
*   Email, chat, or fill out forms
*   Send in feedback

This can include:

*   Your full name
*   Your email
*   Your phone number
*   Where to send stuff and billing info
*   Payment info (our payment partners keep that safe)
*   Anything else you feel like sharing with us

**b. Info We Grab Automatically**

When you use our stuff, we automatically grab some info, like:

*   Your IP address
*   What kind of browser you're using
*   What kind of device you're using
*   When you used our stuff
*   Which pages you looked at
*   How you use our stuff

We use cookies and other tools to grab this. You can turn off cookies in your browser if you want.

**3. How We Use Your Info**

We use your personal info to:

*   Run our Services
*   Process payments
*   Answer questions and give you support
*   Tell you about updates or changes
*   Make our products and website better
*   Keep things secure and stop fraud
*   Do what the law requires

We might also use grouped or anonymous data for research.

**4. Legal Stuff (For Europe &amp; UK Users)**

If you're in Europe (EEA) or the UK, here's why we use your data:

*   **You said it was okay:** When you give us info or agree to get emails.
*   **We need it to do what we promised:** To keep our agreement with you.
*   **The law says we have to:** When the law makes us do it.
*   **It's in our interest:** For things like improving our services, as long as it's fair to you.

**5. How We Share Info**

We don't sell your personal info.

We might share it with:

*   Service people (like payment companies, cloud storage, and support tools)
*   Partners (if we're working on something together)
*   The police (if the law says we have to)
*   If our company gets bought (we'll let you know)

Everyone we share with has to keep your data safe.

**6. Cookies and Tracking**

Cookies help us make things better for you. We use:

*   Cookies that make the site work
*   Cookies that tell us how people use the site
*   Cookies that help us show you ads

You can change your cookie settings in your browser.

**7. How Long We Keep Your Data**

We only keep your personal info as long as we need it to do what we said we'd do in this Privacy Policy, follow the law, and sort out problems.

When we don't need it anymore, we'll delete it or make it anonymous.

**8. Your Rights**

You have the right to:

*   See what info we have about you
*   Change or delete your info
*   Tell us to stop using your info in ways you don't like
*   Take back your consent
*   Get your data in a format you can move somewhere else



We'll check who you are and get back to you within the legal timeframe.

**9. How We Keep Your Data Safe**

We use security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

But no security is perfect, so we can't promise it's 100% safe.

**10. Where Your Data Goes**

Your info might be sent to other countries with different data laws.

We'll make sure any transfers follow the rules and keep your data safe.

**11. Kids' Privacy**

Our stuff is not for kids under 13 (or 16 in some places).

We don't knowingly grab info from kids. If we find out we have a kid's info, we'll delete it ASAP.

**12. Links to Other Sites**

We might have links to other sites, but we're not responsible for what they do.

Check their privacy policies before you give them your info.

**13. Changes to This Policy**

We might change this Privacy Policy from time to time.

We'll post any changes on this page with a new Last Updated date. We might also email you or put a notice on our website.
